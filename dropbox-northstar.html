Dropbox North Star2016

Creating a vision for the Productivity org

Overview

Dropbox product teams are split into 3 main areas: Productivity, Teams and Monetization. I helped lead and create this North Star video for the Productivity team. We created this video to tell a holistic story about how all the work the sub-teams of Productivity were doing would come together to help our users. It was shared throughout the company and played at several all-hands meetings.
