Overview

While working at Dropbox in 2016, I lead and managed the File Collaboration design team in San Francisco. I was responsible for working with other File Collaboration leads (Product and Engineering) to help the teams within File Collaboration to strategize and execute on a product plan that supports Dropbox’s broader initiatives. One of those initiatives was to let users create shared folders on mobile.
