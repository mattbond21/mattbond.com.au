About me

Hi! I’m an Australian product designer in San Francisco. I’ve spent time at some great companies including Dropbox for 1 year where I lead the File Collaboration team, 4 years at Atlassian where I designed the Git repository management tool Bitbucket Server, created the Atlassian Design Guidelines, and worked on growth and engagement for HipChat. Before Atlassian, I graduated University in Australia before working for digital agencies for 5 years as a designer and web developer.

Checkout my resume on LinkedIn and my writing about design on Medium.
